    About Us

    Dr Ladder -
    A Healthcare Service Provider

    PAMA Network was founded in 2007 by a young, vibrant & dynamic team of professionals with rich experience in their respective corporate careers. Our experienced team of professionals has expertise in a broad range of specialties including Recruitment, Leadership Development, Campus Recruitments, Payroll Management, and Training& Development.
    Dr Ladder is a part of PAMA Network which caters recruitment services for Healthcare Industry, and to ensure that all individual needs are met promptly and efficiently every time, ultimately resulting in an improved healthcare system in India & Overseas. With many unadvertised positions available on our database and exclusive supplier contracts to multiple hospitals, we offer doctors the best choice for finding medical jobs in India & Overseas.
    In this highly revered and lifesaving profession, Indian medicos have carved a place for themselves in all the international medical industry. Their intelligence, diligence, skill and genuine compassion for the profession make them the preferred choice of the healthcare centres around the world. We, at Dr Ladder, enable the recruiters to absorb candidates with high level of professionalism and analytical and innovative approach.
